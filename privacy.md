# Privacy Policy

AdBoost respects your privacy completely:

- **No data collection**: We don't collect any personal information
- **No analytics or tracking**: No usage data is gathered or transmitted
- **No network requests**: The extension works entirely offline
- **Client-side only**: All ad injection happens locally in your browser
- **No storage**: Nothing is saved or persisted between sessions

The extension simply injects satirical fake advertisements into web pages for entertainment purposes. Your browsing data never leaves your device.
